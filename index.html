🚫 NEVER BUY PATTERNS
🪦 Instant Rug NEVER
  • Price down >70% in under 48h
  • Liquidity under $2K
  • Dev wallet emptied at launch
  • Sells 3–10x outnumber buys
💀 Pump & Dump NEVER
  • Sharp spike followed by h24 < -50%
  • Volume spikes but liquidity didn't grow
  • H1 was up 100%+, now crashing
  • Coordinated sell-off pattern
🐳 Whale Trap NEVER
  • FDV/Liquidity ratio >200x
  • Low liquidity (<$20K) = trapped
  • 1-5 wallets hold majority supply
  • Exit liquidity for whales only
🚀 RUNNER SIGNALS
🚀 Runner WATCH
  • 24h change >80% with volume growth
  • Buys outnumber sells consistently
  • Liquidity growing alongside price
  • Multiple buy waves (not 1 spike)
💎 Survivor POSSIBLE
  • 30+ days old, still has liquidity
  • Weathered multiple corrections
  • Price held >-30% long term
  • Community still transacting
⚡ Fresh Launch CAUTION
  • Under 24h old — no pattern yet
  • Watch buy/sell ratio first
  • Check if liquidity locked
  • Most fresh tokens become rugs
📐 THE EDGE

Based on pattern data:

→ ~70-80% of new Solana tokens rug or die in <30 days

→ Runners typically show gradual vol increase before price move

→ Red flag: FDV > 50x liquidity = exit trap

→ Green flag: Liq growing = organic demand

→ Buy pressure >60% sustained = momentum trade
